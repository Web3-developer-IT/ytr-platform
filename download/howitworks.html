Simple Process

How It Works

Whether you want to rent a vehicle or earn from yours, we've made the process simple, secure, and seamless.

1

Search & Discover

Browse thousands of vehicles across South Africa. Filter by location, type, price, and features to find your perfect match. Our smart search helps you discover vehicles that fit your exact needs.

Advanced Filters Map View Host Approval Price Comparison
2

Request to Book

Found the perfect match? Send a booking request directly to the host. Once they review your dates and approve the stay, your spot is secured. Our system handles the details so you can focus on the trip.

Host Approval Secure Payments Flexible Dates
3

Pick Up & Drive

Meet the owner, complete the handover checklist, and you're ready to go! Our website guides you through the entire process. Document the vehicle condition with photos for peace of mind.

Digital Handover Photo Documentation 24/7 Support
4

Return & Review

Return the vehicle at the agreed time and location. Complete the return checklist together with the owner. Share your experience to help the community!

Easy Returns Rating System Trip History
1

Listing Review & Approval (No Instant Publishing)

Yours To Rent prioritizes trust and safety. That means every listing is reviewed before it goes live.

Why We Review Listings

South Africa’s market can be unpredictable. Trust is built through verification, not speed.
This review process ensures:

Vehicle accuracy Quality of photos Legitimacy of the owner Safety and reliability for renters
2

Deposit-Backed Booking Requests

Pickup / Dropoff locations are only confirmed once deposit has been paid. This significantly reduces cancellations and increases rental reliability.

What Happens After Submission?

You submit your vehicle details We review the listing for accuracy Once approved, your vehicle is published You receive visibility
3

Hand Over

Meet your renter at the agreed location. Use our website to complete the digital handover checklist and document the vehicle condition. It's quick and professional.

Digital Checklist Photo Proof Contract Signing GPS Tracking
4

Get Paid

Receive payment directly to your bank account within 48 hours of trip completion. Track your earnings, view analytics, and grow your rental business with our owner dashboard.

Fast Payouts Earnings Dashboard Tax Reports Performance Analytics

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.



To rent a vehicle on Yours To Rent, you need a valid South African driver's license (or international license for foreign visitors), a valid ID or passport, and a credit or debit card for payment. Some hosts may have additional requirements which will be listed on the vehicle page.

Our standard cancellation policy allows free cancellation up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup time. Cancellations within 24 hours may receive a 50% refund. Some vehicles may have different policies set by the host, which will be clearly displayed on the listing page before you book.

Earnings vary based on your vehicle type, location, and availability. On average, hosts earn between R5,000 to R15,000 per month. Luxury and specialty vehicles can earn significantly more. Our smart pricing tool helps you optimize your rates based on market demand.

In case of an accident, contact our 24/7 support line immediately. Document the scene with photos and get a police report if necessary. We'll guide you through the claims process and coordinate with the host to resolve the situation as smoothly as possible.

The minimum rental period is typically 1 day (24 hours). Some hosts may set longer minimum rental periods, especially for luxury or specialty vehicles. Weekly and monthly rentals often come with discounted rates. Check the specific vehicle listing for exact terms.
