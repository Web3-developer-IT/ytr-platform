Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Overview

Yours To Rent (“YTR”, “we”, “us”) operates a marketplace connecting vehicle hosts and renters. This policy describes how we process personal data when you use our website and services.

2. Data we collect

  • Account details (name, email, phone where provided).
  • Booking and listing information needed to operate the platform.
  • Verification documents when you use identity or vehicle checks.
  • Technical data such as IP address, device type, and cookies (see our Cookie Policy).

3. How we use data

We use data to provide and improve the service, process bookings, communicate with you, verify accounts, prevent fraud, and comply with law.

4. Sharing

We may share information with service providers who assist our operations (hosting, email, analytics), with other users where needed to complete a booking (for example host and renter contact for a trip), or when required by law.

5. Your rights

Depending on applicable law (including POPIA in South Africa), you may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, and object to certain processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Contact support for privacy requests.

6. Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes above, including legal, accounting, and dispute-resolution requirements.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the platform or by email where appropriate.
