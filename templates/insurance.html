Insurance FAQ

Find answers to common insurance questions.

In case of an accident, ensure everyone's safety first. Contact emergency services if needed, then report the incident through our app or call our 24/7 hotline. Document the scene with photos and exchange information with any other parties involved. Your coverage will handle the rest based on your selected protection plan.

Yes, basic third-party liability insurance is included with every rental at no additional cost. However, we strongly recommend upgrading to our Standard or Premium plans for comprehensive coverage including collision damage waiver and theft protection.

Our insurance does not cover: damage caused while driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs, damage from off-road driving (unless in an approved 4x4 vehicle), mechanical breakdowns due to driver negligence, loss of personal belongings (unless you have Premium coverage), and damage to the vehicle's interior caused by smoking or pets.

In some cases, your personal car insurance or credit card may provide rental car coverage. Contact your insurance provider to confirm. If you choose to use your own insurance, you'll need to provide proof of coverage before the rental. Note that our basic third-party liability coverage will still apply.

The excess is the amount you're responsible for in case of a claim. With Basic coverage, the excess is R15,000. Standard coverage reduces this to R5,000, and Premium coverage offers zero excess. The excess amount is held as a security deposit at the start of your rental.
