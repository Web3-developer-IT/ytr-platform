Turn Your Vehicle Into a Real Income Stream

Yours To Rent is built to help everyday vehicle owners earn extra income using what they already own. This is not about hype or unrealistic promises — it’s about creating real financial opportunities for people who need them most.

1

Clear Pricing & Deposit Visibility

Before requesting a vehicle, renters see a full cost breakdown — the rental price, required refundable deposit, and how funds are handled. Transparency is built into every transaction.

Example: If a vehicle is listed at R550, the required refundable deposit is twice the rental price. All amounts are shown before any commitment is made.

Full cost breakdown Refundable deposit No hidden fees Secure transactions
2

Deposit Paid Into Holding Account

Once a rental request is submitted, the total amount is placed into a secure holding account. Using the R550 example, the renter pays R1,650 upfront (rental + deposit).

Funds are held securely and are only released in stages once conditions are met.

Holding account system Owner approval required Fair fund control
3

Vehicle Collection & Payment Release

When the renter collects the vehicle, the owner immediately receives half of the rental amount. In this example, R225 is released after handover documentation.

Once the renter confirms the vehicle matches the listing, the remaining rental balance is released to the owner.

Condition verification Partial payout Confirmation-based release Digital records
4

Return Vehicle & Deposit Refund

After the vehicle is returned and inspected, the renter receives their full deposit back if the vehicle is returned in the agreed condition.

Any issues are handled transparently, with clear documentation for both parties.

Deposit refund Fair inspection Trust resolution
1

Free Listing & Owner-Controlled Pricing

Listing your vehicle is completely free. You choose your rental price, change it anytime, and keep full control over your earnings.

The platform charges a simple 10% fee per completed rental — you keep 90%.

No listing fees Adjustable pricing 90% owner earnings Transparent fee
2

Deposit-Backed Booking Requests

Owners only receive requests from renters who have already paid a deposit. This significantly reduces cancellations and increases rental reliability.

Serious renters only Reduced no-shows Owner approval Secure transactions
3

Documented Handover & Confirmation

Vehicle condition is documented at handover. Once the renter confirms the vehicle matches the listing, the remaining rental amount is released automatically.

Digital checklist Photo evidence Secure release Dispute prevention
4

Earnings Payout & Fee Deduction

After rental completion, a 10% platform fee is automatically deducted. For a R550 rental, R55 is deducted and the remaining amount is paid to the owner.

Every transaction is recorded clearly for full financial transparency.

Automatic calculation Owner payout Earnings tracking Clear records

Requirements

What You Need

Getting started is easy - here's what you'll need

Valid ID OR Driver’s License

Only one is required — not both. This supports owners who may have inherited a vehicle, share a vehicle, or do not personally drive it.

Proof of Ownership or Authorization

You must prove that you own the vehicle or have authorization to list it. This can be:

Registration papers in your name

An authorized letter from the registered owner

Quality Photos

You will need clear photos of your vehicle from different angles, including interior and exterior.

Optional Verification

In some cases, we may request additional verification for trust and safety. This may include:

Additional photos

A short verification video

Proof of authorization


This is not meant to be intrusive —
it’s to protect both owners and renters.
