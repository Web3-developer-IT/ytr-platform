Our Verification Process

We thoroughly verify every member of our community to ensure a safe and trustworthy platform.

Identity Verification

Users must submit a valid government-issued ID (driver's license, passport, or national ID) which is verified using advanced technology.

Phone Verification

A verified phone number ensures we can reach you for important updates and security alerts.

Email Verification

Confirmed email addresses help us keep your account secure and send important notifications.

Background Checks

For additional security, we conduct background checks on users who want to become vehicle hosts.

Community Guidelines

Our community standards ensure a respectful and safe experience for everyone.

Respect & Honesty

Treat all members with respect. Be honest in your communications and listings.

Be Punctual

Arrive on time for pickups and returns. Communicate if there are any delays.

Keep Vehicles Clean

Return vehicles in the same condition you received them - clean inside and out.

Fuel Policy

Return vehicles with the same fuel level as when you picked them up.

No Smoking

Smoking is prohibited in all vehicles unless explicitly stated otherwise by the owner.

Document Condition

Take photos before and after each rental to document the vehicle's condition.

Emergency Support

In case of an emergency, accident, or safety concern, contact our emergency support line immediately. We're here to help 24/7.
