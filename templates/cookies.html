Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help remember preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how the service is used.

How we use them

We use cookies and similar storage for:

  • Essential — session, security, and load balancing (required for the site to function).
  • Preferences — choices such as language or UI settings where available.
  • Analytics — aggregated usage to improve performance and product (where enabled).

Your choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings (block, delete, or alert). Blocking essential cookies may limit sign-in or booking features.

More detail

Personal data related to cookies is described in our Privacy Policy. Questions? Reach us via Contact.
