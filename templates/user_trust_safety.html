Safety Features

From booking to return, we've implemented multiple layers of safety to protect our community.

Identity Verification

Every user undergoes rigorous ID verification, including driver's license validation, facial recognition, and background checks to ensure a trusted community.

Vehicle Inspections

All listed vehicles must pass our safety standards. We encourage digital pre-trip inspections with photo documentation for transparency.

Secure Payments

All transactions are processed through our secure platform with bank-level encryption. Funds are held safely until trips are completed.

In-App Messaging

Communicate safely through our platform. All messages are recorded and can be used for dispute resolution if needed.
