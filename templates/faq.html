Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about renting, hosting, verification, and how we’re building the product — without fake stats.

Browse listings, open a vehicle you like, choose dates, complete verification if prompted, and submit a booking request. The host approves before the trip is treated as confirmed.

Use List vehicle from your account, add photos and pricing, and submit for review. Admins may verify documents before the listing goes live.

Verification reduces fraud and helps protect both hosts and renters. Status appears in your account; we don’t show fabricated “trust scores.”

Messages are tied to a listing and the other party — use Messages in your account after you’re logged in. You can also reach support via Contact.

Card capture and automated payouts are coming soon. Current flows focus on booking records and transparency — check the Services page for what’s live.

We’re building structured protection options with partners — nothing is presented as guaranteed coverage until it’s live in product. See Insurance for the current narrative.
